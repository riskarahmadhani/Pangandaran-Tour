Travel Destination

TWA Cagar Alam

At TWA Cagar Alam, visitors can see various kinds of flora and fauna and a number of cultural heritages.

Citumang

At Citumang, visitors can do water activities other than swimming, namely body rafting.

Batu Hiu Beach

At Batu Hiu beach, visitors can see the beauty of the sea off the Indian Ocean from the coral hills.
