Travel Destination

TWA Cagar Alam

At TWA Cagar Alam, visitors can see various kinds of flora and fauna and a number of cultural heritages.

Madasari Beach

At Madasari Beach, visitors can see the beauty of the waves hitting the rocks.

Citumang

At Citumang, visitors can do water activities other than swimming, namely body rafting.

Kampung Turis

At Kampung Turis, visitors can enjoy a variety of marine cuisine accompanied by the waves.

Sagati Park

At Sagati Park, visitors can see art performances and batik ecoprints.

Museum Nyamuk

At the Museum Nyamuk, visitors can learn about mosquitoes.
